Welcome to ABES Engineering College

ABES Engineering College, established in 2000, is a premier institution located in Ghaziabad, India. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Known for its commitment to academic excellence, ABES Engineering College boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life. The institution emphasizes research, innovation, and industry collaboration to prepare students for successful careers in their respective fields. With a focus on holistic development, ABES Engineering College aims to nurture skilled professionals who can contribute effectively to society and the global workforce.
